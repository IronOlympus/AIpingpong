Instructions

  1. Keep your laptop screen straight
  2. Move yourself, keep 4 feet away from the laptop
  3. Move your right wrist in an ascending and descending manner,red dot should appear on your right wrist
  4. Press the play button and again move yourself and keep 4 feet from your laptop
  5. The red paddle is your paddle that will move as per the movement of your right wrist
